Who We Are

At MotorPride Kenya, we're more than just a car dealership - we're your gateway to automotive excellence. Founded with a vision to transform Kenya's vehicle import industry, we've become a trusted name for discerning customers seeking quality, reliability, and value.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every Kenyan deserves access to premium vehicles without the premium price tag. Through strategic partnerships with international suppliers and a meticulous selection process, we've turned this belief into reality for thousands of satisfied customers.

Today, we stand as a beacon of trust in an industry often clouded by uncertainty, offering not just vehicles, but peace of mind and a driving experience that exceeds expectations.
