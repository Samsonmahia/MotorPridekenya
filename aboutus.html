Who We Are

MotorPride Kenya is a trusted motor vehicle importer and dealer based in Kenya. With years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on delivering quality vehicles, excellent customer service, and a hassle-free buying experience. We are committed to helping clients find the perfect car while ensuring full transparency and trust.

What We Do

At MotorPride Kenya, we specialize in the importation and sale of both new and pre-owned vehicles sourced directly from trusted global markets including Japan, the UK, and others. Our goal is to make high-quality cars accessible and affordable to all our clients. We offer car financing, logbook loans, asset financing through reputable financial partners, and vehicle trade-ins. For those with specific needs, we provide personalized vehicle sourcing to ensure clients receive their exact preferences.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality vehicles to all clients with transparency and professionalism. We are driven to simplify car ownership by offering smart solutions, dependable customer support, and unmatched integrity.

Our Vision

To be Kenya’s most trusted and customer-focused car dealership—delivering value and peace of mind to every customer. We envision a future where every car purchase is simple, satisfying, and transparent.

Why Choose Us

MotorPride Kenya offers a seamless and trusted experience for car buyers and sellers by prioritizing convenience, transparency, and smart technology. Trusted by thousands of happy clients across Kenya, we offer flexible and affordable payment plans, direct imports from top markets like Japan and the UK, and a customer-first approach in every interaction. All our vehicles are fully inspected and verified, ensuring quality, value, and peace of mind at every step.
