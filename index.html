Who We Are

Motorpride Kenya began as a dream to redefine how Kenyans buy, sell, and experience vehicles. From humble beginnings in Kisumu, we’ve grown into a trusted digital dealership platform—blending local identity with global innovation. Our mission? To empower every Kenyan with access to reliable, stylish, and affordable vehicles, backed by transparency and tech.
